In the next few years, we expect very rapid growth in sustainable fixing in Asia. Indeed, we are at the tipping point right now. Close to 20% of gross issuance in Asia so far this year have been in green, social, sustainability and sustainability linked bond. And now Asian issuance are the largest source of E-SG bond across emerging markets. We expect E-SG bond issuance to continue to increase. This development will offer more choices for investors who want to allocate their capital, not only to produce financial return but also put their money to achieve certain objectives, such as diversity or greening the economies. We see opportunities from reliable energy sector and green bond issues from property companies. One of the investment opportunities is to identify names with improving transparency and governance as well as those with a thoughtful approach to transit and manage their climate risk. For example, as it will become harder for co-mining companies to secure funding going forward, credit risk will increase. In comparison, energy companies with a proper transition framework will be more competitive. When we look at an investment, we evaluate the overall E-SG quality of a company. Then it is a limited correlation between the issuance of a green bond and the overall sustainability of the company. These considerations are now part of our DNA in our credit investment analysis. At the portfolio level, it is not straightforward to attribute the effect of environmental, social and governance through financial return. E-S and G drivers are somewhat related and there are many variables in the portfolio's financial performance. We developed the SubstankX to tackle this. SubstankX is our proprietary tool to identify a company's sustainability impact on society and translate them into financial terms. For example, SubstankX will be able to quantify and tell us a company's social value as a percentage of revenues. If a company has a score of positive 5%, it means that for every hundred dollars of sales the company is making an overall positive impact on society equivalent to $5. This is now a key decision-making tool for our portfolio construction. As an asset manager, we are committed to sustainability. As a part of our investors, we are here to help them understand the E-SG data porn for individual holdings, as well as their sustainability contribution from their investment.
