The Nautixis Investment Management Managers' Graduate Challenge was launched last year for final year students and to give them the opportunity to manage in real-life condition a portfolio over a three months period. The first edition was open to French students and we decided thanks to the success of the first edition to broaden and open it to more global, including the UK, Europe and the US. What we offer to the students is a clear framework in understanding what investors need, how they need to apply, how they need to approach portfolio management. We then offer them training sessions both on the operational side as well as the portfolio management side and then in real life when this challenges start we have the each team supported by one specific mental from our affiliates, investment experts. Bear in mind the actual trends we are seeing in the industry, we really wanted the students and the overall investment guidelines to reflect the trends we are seeing in the industry. Therefore, emphasises one was put on team diversity. We also asked for the students to reflect on ING and how that could be applied to their portfolios. In terms of number, the second edition of the sixes investment manager, Gajit Challenge, enabled us to receive over 42 teams submission, representing 200 students. We then selected 12 teams to participate to the challenge. The 12 teams have started managing as of February 1st. After the first week where they have discovered the operational platform, how to place orders and lies with their mentor to finalise their initial portfolio, they will then progress during the life of the challenge. We will definitely update you as they progress. Stay tuned.
