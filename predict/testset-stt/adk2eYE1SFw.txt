With the global sustainable equity strategy, we want to offer a solution for investors who want to invest sustainably and achieve returns better than the broad market. That's our double objective. We want to and need to outperform the MSCR world and at the same time we want to and need to do that with a much better social and environmental profile. For that we have developed an in-house proprietary ESG rating system and also used an innovative, sophisticated carbon footprint methodology to make sure that our strategy is aligned with a 2-degree global warming scenario. This basically means that if the world would be organized like our strategy, the world would be warming up no more than 2 degrees. We strongly believe that the market is significantly underappreciating the growth opportunities coming from the fact that our world is constantly changing. At the same time we also believe that ESG risks are not properly priced in and that gives us an evaluation opportunity to outperform. We believe that our economy is slowly transitioning to a much more sustainable model. That transition is driven by demographic, environmental, technological and governance-related trends. We invest in companies that we believe will benefit from those trends because they offer solutions for those trends to happen, but also companies within that group that are well managed, don't take irresponsible risks and are trading at a significant discount to what we believe is their intrinsic value. The result is a high-convection portfolio with approximately 50 names. We have a low turnover and a high active share. Compared to the broad market, we have a small bias towards quality and growth companies, although we also invest in some more traditional value names as well.
